Game Objective

Guess the hidden five-letter word in six tries or fewer. Work together with other players in real-time!

How It Works

After each guess, tiles change color to give you clues:

W
Green: Letter is correct and in the right position
O
Yellow: Letter is in the word but wrong position
R
Gray: Letter is not in the word

Scoring System

Points are based on Scrabble letter values. Green letters earn full points, yellow letters earn half points (with the remaining half awarded when they turn green).

  • +3 bonus for guessing the correct word
  • -3 penalty if your final guess is wrong
  • -1 penalty for repeat guesses with no new letters
